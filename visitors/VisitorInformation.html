Parking

Parking can be located either on the streets surrounding the Maxwell (coin-only meters) or at a nearby parking garage.

Street Parking

You can find street parking all around downtown. All downtown street parking is metered ($1.25/Hour) but they are free from 9pm-9am every day and completely free on Sundays.

Parking Garages

There are several parking garages near the Maxwell.

Well Fargo: 837 Cherry St, Des Moines, IA 50309

Just east of the Maxwell, across 9th street, is the Wells Fargo employee parking garage. This lot is open to the public from 5pm-3am every day, and on weekends from 5pm, Friday to 3am Monday.

Entering the building

The entrance to the Maxwell is on the south side of the southeast corner of the building.

There is a keypad to the left of the door, enter [1, 4, 8, 5] and the door will unlock. Once you are inside the building, there is an elevator across from the mailboxes or a stairwell just to the right. Our apartment is located on the third floor, down the hall from the elevator/stairs, and just around the corner on your right.
